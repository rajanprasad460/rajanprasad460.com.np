About Me

I am a Post Doctoral Fellow at Khalifa University with a PhD in Mechanical Engineering. My research focuses on developing innovative bio-robotic assistive exoskeletons for stroke patient rehabilitation and studying the influence of biofeedback on reducing mental stress. I have extensive experience in dynamics, control systems, and rehabilitation robotics.

My work spans from cable-driven exoskeletons for rehabilitation to underwater hybrid manipulators and stress analysis through bio-signals. I'm passionate about creating engineering solutions that directly benefit human health and wellbeing.
